As a staff, I can skip the queue for the next customer if the previous customer doesn't arrive on time so that another customer doesn't have to wait too long.

1) The product shall show the list of queue of the customer to the staff.
2) The product shall skip the queue of the customer by he/she doesn't arrive on time.
3) THe product shall show the customer information to only authorized staff.

As a customer, I can queue even I don't physically waiting in the line so that I can do anything else while waiting.

1) The product shall show the current queue to the customer(real-time).
2) The product shall be mobility for customer to check his/her queue information. 
3) The product shall notify customer if reach his/her queue.

As a staff, I can estimate wait time for a customer so that I can tell the customer if he/she asked.

1) The product shall show the estimate wait time for all queue to the staff.
2) The product shall show the list of queue of the customer to the staff.
3) The product shall show the real-time estimate wait time if the staff skip the customer queue.
